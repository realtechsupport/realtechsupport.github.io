Office Plant, 1997-1998

office greenery in a networked world

Office Plant was an investigation into then nascent email space. Office Plant was modeled to fit into the same emotional space as generic office greenery and designed to participate in office life. Office Plant was able to read a person's email and react to the content of the messages using text classification techniques.
